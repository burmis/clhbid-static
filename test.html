CLHbid.com is a proven online tender platform that solves the shortcomings associated with existing methods available for sellers of agricultural property.

Your farm or ranch represents a lifetime of work. As the saying goes “a farmer or rancher punches in at 8 years old and doesn’t punch out until they retire.” Few jobs on the farm are completed only once in a lifetime. The exit is one of those jobs. One of the most important, if not the most important, comes without a test drive. In the past, often the exit came with the undue stress related to single-handedly dealing with competing buyers at the kitchen table, often neighbours, friends or even relatives. Times have changed. If your goal is to maximize value for a lifetime of work while ensuring all buyers are treated fairly, you will want CLHbid.com on your exit team. The professional team at CLHbid.com has made the drive many times.

Your Privacy is Guaranteed

When bidding on CLHbid.com your name and any personal identifiable information never shows up during the bidding process. Bidder’s names on the CLHbid.com system are kept in confidence. It is only if you are highest bidder when the auction has ended that your name will ever be disclosed to the Seller.

This Auction has a Dynamic Closing Time

Closing times posted on CLHbid.com are subject to Dynamic Closing.

If any bids are placed within 2 minutes of the scheduled auction closing time, the whole auction will extend by an additional 2 minutes. The auction will continue with the new scheduled closing time.

Dynamic Closing helps to ensure that all interested bidders get a chance to place a winning bid for any item they are interested in.
